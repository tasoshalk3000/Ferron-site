Frequently asked

The basics

What is Ferron?

Ferron is a two-app system that turns your iPhone into a customizable control surface for your Mac. Eight beautifully designed pages — app shortcuts, a trackpad, keyboard, media controls, profiles, macros, clipboard, and live system stats — let you control your Mac from your phone without ever reaching for the keyboard.

Do I need both apps to use Ferron?

Yes. Ferron requires both:

  • The Ferron iPhone app (free)
  • The Ferron Mac companion app (one-time purchase)

The iPhone app is the control surface, and the Mac app handles configuration and executes commands. Without the Mac companion running, the iPhone app will show "Searching for Mac…"

My iPhone shows "Searching for Mac…" — what's wrong?

This means your iPhone hasn't found a Mac running Ferron on your local network. Check:

  • Both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network
  • The Ferron Mac app is open (look for the F icon in your menu bar)
  • Wi-Fi is enabled on both devices — Multipeer Connectivity uses Wi-Fi even in peer-to-peer mode
  • Bluetooth is enabled on both devices (used as a fallback)
  • Your firewall isn't blocking Ferron — on Mac, check System Settings → Network → Firewall

If you've checked all of the above, try restarting both apps. If problems persist, get in touch below and we'll help you troubleshoot.

Does Ferron need an internet connection?

No. Ferron is local-first by design. All communication happens directly between your iPhone and Mac over Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or peer-to-peer Wi-Fi. Nothing goes through any server. You can use Ferron fully offline as long as both devices can reach each other on a local network.

Does Ferron collect or transmit any of my data?

No. Ferron does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All your configuration — profiles, shortcuts, macros, clipboard items — is stored locally on each device. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, no tracking.

See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Can I customize what's on my iPhone?

Yes. From the Mac companion app, you can:

  • Choose which Mac apps appear in your App Shortcuts grid
  • Build custom profiles that launch apps and set up your workspace
  • Define keyboard shortcuts and multi-step macros
  • Toggle which pages are visible on the iPhone

All customization happens on the Mac and syncs to your iPhone automatically when both devices are paired.

What macOS and iOS versions do I need?

Ferron requires:

  • iPhone: iOS 18 or later
  • Mac: macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later

We require recent OS versions to take advantage of the latest SwiftUI and Multipeer Connectivity APIs, which keep latency low and the experience smooth.

Is there a subscription?

No. Ferron is a one-time purchase. The Mac app is paid, and the iPhone companion app is free. There are no in-app purchases, no ads, and no subscriptions. Buy it once, own it forever.

Why is the iPhone app landscape-only?

Ferron's control surface is designed for ergonomic two-handed use, where you hold your iPhone like a controller. Landscape lets us spread controls comfortably across the screen — bigger buttons, easier reach, and more space for live information. Portrait would make the surface feel cramped.

How do I request a refund?

All Ferron purchases go through Apple, so refunds are handled through the standard App Store refund process. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your Ferron purchase to request a refund. Apple typically processes refund requests within 48 hours.

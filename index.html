Everything in one place

Eight ways to control your Mac.

Swipe between pages on your iPhone. Every page is fully customizable from the Mac app.

01 — App Shortcuts

Your apps, one tap away.

Launch or focus any Mac app instantly. Pull real icons straight from your Mac, or set your own.

02 — Trackpad

A full trackpad, on your phone.

Move the cursor, click, scroll, and use real multi-finger gestures mapped to anything you want.

03 — Keyboard & Dictation

Type from anywhere.

Use the native iOS keyboard to type on your Mac from the couch, or tap once to dictate by voice.

04 — Media Controls

Play, skip, volume, brightness.

Big, easy-to-hit buttons for transport, volume, and display brightness — exactly where you can reach them.

05 — Custom Profiles

One tap. Whole workflow.

A profile launches a complete scene — open your apps, set volume and brightness, jump to the right space.

06 — Shortcuts & Macros

Hotkeys. Context-aware.

Universal shortcuts plus a panel that adapts to whichever app you're focused on. Chain steps into macros.

07 — Clipboard Manager

Every copy, on hand.

Your recent clipboard items appear on your phone. Tap to paste; pin the ones you want to keep around.

08 — System Monitor

Your Mac's vitals at a glance.

Live gauges for CPU, GPU, RAM, disk, network and power. Tap any one to see the top processes.
